OpenWorld Departments

OpenWorld has a variety of departments that serve the city and the county


Primary Departments

The River City Police Department is committed to protecting the public with professionally trained Police officers. We want to work closely with the public and businesses and to achieve the best possible outcome of any incident. We also want to make communities safer by upholding the law fairly and firmly with the actions we take.


Our mission is to improve the quality of life by providing a safe and secure environment for the public. We take pride in providing the community with a professional law enforcement service, safe community engagement events and community partnerships. We also pride ourselves by providing the community with transparency and accountability.


The mission of Liberty State Police is to insure a safe environment for all visitors and residents throughout the state. LSP personnel are trained and held to a high standard to insure the safety and knowledge of traffic laws. Although focused primarily on the enforcement of traffic laws we support the efforts of all local safety agencies to reduce crime and apprehend those who commit them. LSP personnel will uphold our mission, professionalism and compassion.


Secondary Departments

The River City Fire and Rescue is one of the most vital departments inside Open World. They manage the safety of all citizens by extinguishing fires or providing aid to those in need. They can perform rescue operations, including water rescues, and overall help keep Liberty County safe.


Liberty County Communications is composed of specially trained dispatchers who are ready to deploy emergency services to citizens across the state efficiently and accurately. We are committed to serving the citizens with the highest professional standards.


Our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation options that enhance the mobility, economic growth, and quality of life for all citizens. Through partnerships and collaboration, we strive to create a transportation network that is resilient, reliable, and responsive to the needs of our communities, now and in the future.
